About Mirajane

Mirajane Strauss is one of the strongest and most beloved characters from the Fairy Tail Guild. Known for her kind and gentle personality, she is a caring older sister to her siblings, Elfman and Lisanna. However, she is also an S-Class mage, possessing immense strength and power when it comes to battle.
